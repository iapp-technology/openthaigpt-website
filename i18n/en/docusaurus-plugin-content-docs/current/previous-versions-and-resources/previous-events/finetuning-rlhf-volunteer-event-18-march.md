# Finetuning / RLHF Volunteer Event (18 March)

An announcement, if I may. Hello, this is Dr. Sumeth.

I would like to invite all volunteers interested in Finetuning / RLHF to join the First Meet of the Finetuning / RLHF Volunteer team on Saturday 18 March 2023, from 13:00 to 14:00.

The main goal of this session is to organize all volunteers into smaller working groups and to select a representative for each group who will help drive the Finetuning / RLHF Volunteer effort forward.

## Agenda for the First Meet

* 13:00-13:30 Introduction to Finetuning / RLHF, why it matters, and the current plan
* 13:30-13:40 Overview of the working groups and their responsibilities
* 13:40-13:50 Q&A
* 13:50-14:00 Volunteers sign up for the group of their choice
