# Safety Net Volunteer Event (12 March 19:00-19:45)

An announcement, if I may. Hello, this is Oat from the SafetyNet team.

I would like to invite all volunteers interested in SafetyNet to join the First Meet of the SafetyNet team on Sunday 12 March 2023, from 19:00 to 19:45.

The main goal of this session is to organize all volunteers into smaller working groups and to select a representative for each group who will help drive SafetyNet forward.

## Agenda for the First Meet

* 19:00-19:10 Introduction to SafetyNet, why it matters, and the current plan
* 19:10-19:25 Overview of the working groups and their responsibilities
* 19:25-19:30 QA
* 19:30-19:45 Volunteers sign up for the group of their choice
