# Core-team Volunteer Meeting 19 March 15:30

Hello everyone, this is Dr. C. Tomorrow (Sunday 19 March 2023) there will be a Core Team meeting of the OpenThaiGPT team from 15:30 to 17:30.

All team leaders and the entire coordination team are invited to join, as well as any other volunteers who are interested in sharing their thoughts.

## Meeting Topics

* Meeting to define Core Team responsibilities
* Setting clear shared goals for OpenThaiGPT 1.0.0
  * What architecture?
  * Max\_Length?
  * Will it be multi-modal?
  * Capability goals
    * Can it translate between Thai and English?
    * Can it summarize?
    * Can it perform few-shot learning?
    * Math?
    * Can it handle syllable-level capabilities, such as spoonerisms (คำผวน)?
    * Should it be smart enough to score around 80% on various Entrance exam subjects?
  * Evaluation
  * Connecting to external knowledge sources?
  * Other capabilities that would benefit the Thai economy in the future
* Defining a clear project schedule for each team
* Q/A
* Scheduling the next Core Team meeting
