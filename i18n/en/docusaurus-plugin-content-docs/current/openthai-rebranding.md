# 📢 Announcement: OpenThaiGPT is Renaming to OpenThai

*Announced on 19 July 2026*

The **OpenThaiGPT** project is pleased to officially announce that it is renaming to **OpenThai**, together with the unveiling of a new project logo.

## Reasons for the Name and Logo Change

1. **The word "GPT" cannot be registered as a trademark** — the trademark application for the name OpenThaiGPT was vetoed by OpenAI, which holds the rights to the term GPT.
2. **The previous logo incorporated the Thai national flag** — under the law, a national flag cannot be registered as a trademark.

## The New OpenThai Logo

<figure class="brand-logo"><img src="/img/openthai-logo-full.png" alt="The new OpenThai logo — a cloud with a brain, wearing glasses"><figcaption>The new OpenThai logo</figcaption></figure>

The new logo depicts **a cloud with a brain, wearing glasses**, conveying the idea of being **"the brain of AI for Thailand Cloud"** — artificial intelligence running on Thailand's own cloud, in order to promote **Thailand's AI Sovereignty** in the years ahead.

## What Is Not Changing

- The project remains open source — Free and Opensource for all Thai people, as before.
- Models already released (OpenThaiGPT 1.0, 1.5, 1.6, R1) will keep their original names for the historical record, and remain available for download at [Hugging Face](https://huggingface.co/openthaigpt) as before.
- Past documentation and activities on this website will continue to refer to the name OpenThaiGPT, in accordance with the historical facts of the project.
